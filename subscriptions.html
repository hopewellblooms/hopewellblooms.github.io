Subscriptions

Treat yourself or someone you love to the gift of flowers!

Fresh beautiful blooms to enjoy at home

Giving a gift of a flower subscription is easy.

Fresh bouquets can be enjoyed from April to November.

Recipient can choose to receive the bouquets for 5 consecutive weeks, bi-weekly, or once a month.

Recipient can have a choice of a Thursday or Friday pick-up or delivery day. Delivery is $5 per bouquet.

Gift cards are beautifully wrapped and mailed for your convenience.

A 5-week CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) subscription is only $110. Your gift supports our initiatives to grow beautiful flowers sustainably, feed our pollinators, and maintain biodiversity.

Email us at freshflowers@hopewellblooms.com to get started.

Please note that delivery is for residents of Symmes Township and surrounding areas. Thank you for supporting Hopewell Blooms as your choice for locally grown fresh flowsers.
